What is Calisthenics?

  • Calisthenics is a bodyweight exercise using minimal or no equipment.
  • Calisthenics was practiced as far back as in Ancient Greece.
    Both men and women practiced various forms of this sport
  • Calisthenics is both physical as well as mental strength to
    create both a strong and esthetically pleasing body.

Importance of Calisthenics

  • Calisthenics has many physical and mental benefits like enhancing strength, stamina, agility, mental health and social contacts.
  • Calisthenics has no significant costs to get into.
  • The core principles of Calisthenics are progressive overload, compound exercise, high range of motion and focus on perfect form and technique.
